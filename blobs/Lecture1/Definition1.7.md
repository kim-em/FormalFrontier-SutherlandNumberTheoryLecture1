**Definition 1.7.** For a prime $p$ the *$p$-adic valuation* $v_p \colon \mathbb{Q} \to \mathbb{Z}$ is defined by

$$v_p\!\left(\pm \prod_q q^{e_q}\right) \coloneqq e_p,$$

and we define $v_p(0) \coloneqq \infty$. The *$p$-adic absolute value* on $\mathbb{Q}$ is defined by

$$\lvert x \rvert_p \coloneqq p^{-v_p(x)},$$

where $\lvert 0 \rvert_p = p^{-\infty}$ is understood to be $0$.
